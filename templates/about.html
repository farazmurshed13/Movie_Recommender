About

This app was born from the frustrating feeling of not knowing what movie to watch. Especially during the global pandemic, we spend more and more time at home, doing our part as responsible citizens by social distancing but feeling so bored. Movies are a great way to pass the time, but sometimes you don't feel like endlessly scrolling through Netflix or Hulu, waiting for something to catch your eye.

This app allows groups of friends to collaboratively get movie recommendations by answering a questionnaire via SMS text. We gauge each person's interests based on their answers to the questions and provide a list of suggested movies that the whole group can enjoy.
